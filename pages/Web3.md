- ## PoS vs PoW
	- [[Sep 15th, 2022]]
		- ETH switch from PoW to PoS
		- What is PoW/PoS?
	- ### Pow
		- PoW = Prove of Work
		- A game of calculating a hash function.
		- BTC: About 10~15 minutes grow a new block。
	- ### PoS
		- PoS = Prove of Stake
		- $幣齡(\text{CoinAge}) = 押天數 \times 代幣數量$
		- 幣齡越高，越有機會獲取記帳權。
		- $利息 = 幣齡 \times 代幣年利率 \div 365$
		- Pro
			- 不需要大量能量
			- 不需要礦機
		- Con
			- 富者越富
			- 集中化 => 違反區塊鏈的信念
			- 通貨膨漲
